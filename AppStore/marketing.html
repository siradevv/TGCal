TGCal

Roster PDF to flights, earnings, and calendar workflow for cabin crew and pilots.

What TGCal does

  • Import roster PDFs and parse monthly flights.
  • Show a concise flights list with monthly context.
  • Estimate monthly and per-flight earnings.
  • Add flights to Apple Calendar (including month replacement for updates).
  • Show next-flight destination briefing with travel essentials.

Privacy-first design

TGCal is designed for on-device processing where possible, with no account required and no advertising SDKs.
